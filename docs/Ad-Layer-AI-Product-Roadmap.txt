Executive Summary
Vision

AdLayer AI is a Creative Intelligence Platform that transforms static advertisements into structured, reusable, and automation-ready data models.

By uploading a single advert image (JPG, PNG, PDF, or screenshot), AdLayer AI automatically:

Deconstructs the creative into logical layers

Extracts all textual and visual elements

Reconstructs the advert’s information architecture

Classifies components (headline, CTA, offer, brand, legal, etc.)

Maps everything into a structured schema

Generates a downloadable, production-ready CSV for automation workflows

AdLayer AI bridges the gap between creative design and structured marketing data.
